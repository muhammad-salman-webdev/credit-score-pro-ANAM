How It Works

step 01

Get Started

It’s easy, and only takes a few minutes. First, click the get started button, and register to set up your account.

step 02

Link Your Credit Report

Credit Score Pro automatically imports and analyzes your 3 bureau credit report, finds negative accounts and finds an aggresive dispute strategy. Best of all, Credit Score Pro never puts an inquiry on your credit report!

step 03

AI Selects Items & You Send Letters

AI technology works tirelessly to identify inaccuracies and discrepancies in your credit reports. It converts your credit reports into a digital ledger and analyzes every account across all bureaus and runs thousands of algorithms on every account to identify different errors. Our intuitive platform guides you to select the specific items that need attention, whether they be erroneous late payments, incorrect account details, or other credit report discrepancies and then our AI selects the best dispute reason based on millions of past disputes. In addition, it provides dispute strategy for accounts without visible errors. Once identified, our system generates personalized dispute letters tailored to each item, empowering you to take informed action and set the credit record straight. It’s as easy as click and send!

step 04

Sit Back & Relax

When the credit bureaus receive your sent letters, they will conduct an investigation of the items you challenged.

step 05

Results

Every month Credit Score Pro imports your new 3 bureau credit report and shows which accounts were deleted and your new credit scores. If an account wasn’t deleted, Credit Score Pro will suggest a new strategy for additional disputes.

step 06

Repeat

If the errors are still on your credit report, Credit Score Pro’s AI will apply more pressure with advanced disputing methods. Remember, Credit Score Pro’s AI-powered technology learns based on the results of millions of past disputes and will continue refining dispute strategies.

step 07

Build & Optimize Credit

Building and optimizing your credit is a crucial aspect of your financial journey. Credit Score Pro’s AI creates a credit building plan identifying opportunities for score boosts. Credit Score Pro offers expert insights and tailored strategies to help you enhance your credit profile. We guide you on responsible credit usage, effective debt management, and strategic steps to optimize your credit score. By implementing these personalized recommendations, you can pave the way for a stronger and more secure financial future.
